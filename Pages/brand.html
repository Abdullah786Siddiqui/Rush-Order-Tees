Custom Adidas FAQs

The cost of personalized Adidas apparel varies depending on the product type, customization method, and quantity ordered. Basic embroidery typically starts at $8-12 per item, while screen printing can range from $5-15 per piece. Premium customization options like heat transfer vinyl or specialty inks may cost more. We offer competitive bulk pricing for larger orders, and there are no setup fees for orders over 12 pieces.

We offer several customization methods for Adidas apparel: Embroidery - Perfect for logos, text, and detailed designs with a premium feel. Screen Printing - Ideal for large designs and bulk orders with vibrant colors. Heat Transfer Vinyl - Great for names, numbers, and simple graphics. Direct-to-Garment Printing - Best for complex, full-color designs and photo-realistic images. Simply upload your design or work with our design team to create custom artwork that meets your specifications.
