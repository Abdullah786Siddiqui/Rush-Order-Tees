About Us

RushOrderTees delivers the high-quality custom apparel you want on the deadline you need.

We offer a wide variety of products that can be personalized with logos or artwork including custom t-shirts, polo shirts, hats, custom hoodies, and much more.

Weâ€™re proud to be the official partner of the Philadelphia 76ers and have more than 500,000 satisfied customers, ranging from Fortune 500 companies to small mom-and-pop shops. Whether an order is just one t-shirt or 10,000, we deliver high-quality prints in record time.

History

We started in a garage in 2002. While still in high school, our CEO Mike Nemeroff and his two siblings began screen printing as a way to help provide for their family during a difficult time. They learned the process from the ground up and obsessed over every detail.

The business developed over the years and Rush Order Tees grew into a leader in custom apparel products and became known for delivering the fastest turnaround times and exceptional customer service.
